Christmas Activities in Athens, GA

If you are going to be in Athens, GA during the Holiday season, this webpage will help you choose from several fun and entertaining Holiday related activities that you can access right here in town!

List of Activities

  • Athens Christmas Parade
  • Iceskating at the Classic Center
  • The Nutcrakcer Ballet at the Classic Center
  • The Athens Holiday Market

Information

Athens Christmas Parade

The Downtown Athens parade of lights is on December 6th at 7:00pm. The parade will take place downtown.

Link to Parade of Lights Website

Iceskating at the Classic Center

The Classic Center will have iceskating sessions open to the public starting on November 23rd.

Link to Athens on Ice

Watch a performance of The Nutcracker at the Classic Center

The Nutcracker Ballet will be performed at the Classic Center on December 22nd and 23rd.

Link to buy tickets

The Athens Holiday Market

The Athens Holiday market is December 13th and 14th from 5-9pm and is located in the Big City Bread Courtyard. Here you can shop around from local Athens vendors for some unique handmade Christmas gifts for your loved ones or yourself.
